# ResearchRewards — Paid-to-Learn Research Platform

## Overview

A dark, cozy research hub where users earn money by browsing interest-based content. Revenue from ads is redistributed based on a 14-tier importance hierarchy, rewarding research into life-saving and scientific topics more than entertainment or betting.

## Design

- **Dark & cozy**: Deep Emerald-Carbon Black backgrounds (#00170A-range), warm muted accents, soft rounded cards, comfortable typography.
- **Ambient feel**: Subtle gradients, gentle glows, a "study lounge" atmosphere that invites long sessions
- **Primary accent**: Warm amber colour for Experience Bar earnings and Crimson for Multipliers, Golden Carbon-Black Surrounded rewards indicators Numbers.
